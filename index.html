Information

Ruby is a concise and purely object-oriented programming language, with a Perl-like syntax as well as Smalltalk-like object-oriented programming. Ruby is developed by Japanese programmer Yukihiro "Matz" Matsumoto. The language is often used for web page scripting and smaller console scripts, but it is also applicable to larger programs, even graphic programs, through Tk and RubyCocoa.

The name Ruby
